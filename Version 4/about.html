About Us

Our History

LVinnamon, a global brand specializing in cinnamon rolls, started in 2000 with the opening of its first store. The irresistible taste and aroma of their cinnamon rolls quickly gained popularity, leading to the expansion of branches in various cities. LVinnamon's commitment to quality and friendly service made it a beloved destination for cinnamon enthusiasts. Today, it is a global icon known for its wide range of cinnamon-infused treats and beverages, providing a heavenly experience for customers worldwide.

Company Profile

LVinnamon is a global business specializing in the sale of cinnamon rolls and baked goods. With branches worldwide, we offer a range of delectable treats infused with the perfect blend of cinnamon and sugar. Our menu also includes pastries, muffins, cookies, cakes, and breads, all made with premium ingredients. Complemented by a selection of hot and cold beverages, LVinnamon aims to create moments of indulgence and delight for our customers. We prioritize quality, customer satisfaction, creativity, sustainability, and community engagement. Join us at LVinnamon for an unforgettable culinary experience.
